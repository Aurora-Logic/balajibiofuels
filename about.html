About Us

A revolutionary step towards sustainable energy and rural empowerment through cooperative excellence and ex-servicemen leadership

Building a Sustainable Future Through Collaboration

At Shree Balalji BioFuels, we believe in building a sustainable future through collaboration. Our ethanol plant represents a revolutionary cooperative-driven initiative where procurement, operations, and management are conducted through Venkateshwara Co-operative Power and Agro Processing Ltd.

What makes us unique is the leadership of ex-servicemen, who bring discipline, commitment, and integrity to every aspect of our operations—from agriculture support to raw material procurement and plant management. This approach ensures operational excellence while maintaining our commitment to social responsibility.

Our mission is simple but powerful: to promote clean and green energy, empower Indian farmers, and contribute to India's energy security through sustainable ethanol production.

Our Cooperative Framework

1

Farmer-Owned Operations

All major decisions are made collectively by farmer members, ensuring their interests remain paramount.

2

Transparent Governance

Open books, regular meetings, and democratic decision-making processes ensure complete transparency.

3

Profit Sharing

All surplus revenue is shared among farmer members, ensuring they benefit from the value they create.

4

Continuous Support

Ongoing technical assistance, training, and market support to enhance farmer productivity and income.

Our Mission

To promote clean and green energy, empower Indian farmers, and contribute to India's energy security through sustainable grain-based ethanol production with a cooperative-driven approach.

Our Vision

To be India's leading cooperative in sustainable energy production, setting the benchmark for farmer empowerment, environmental stewardship, and energy independence.

Our Values

Integrity, transparency, sustainability, farmer empowerment, and excellence in operations. We believe in prosperity through cooperation and mutual respect.
