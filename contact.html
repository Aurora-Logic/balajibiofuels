Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

Farmers can join by contacting our farmer helpline or visiting our office. We provide complete guidance on membership requirements, benefits, and the registration process. Our ex-servicemen team will assist with all documentation and onboarding.

We primarily procure maize and rice for ethanol production. Our team provides quality specifications, fair pricing, and guaranteed procurement agreements to ensure farmers have a reliable market for their crops.

We welcome partnerships in technology, distribution, supply chain, and sustainability initiatives. Contact our partnership team to discuss collaboration opportunities that align with our cooperative values and sustainable energy mission.

We provide comprehensive support including high-quality seeds, fertilizers, technical guidance, training programs, direct procurement, fair pricing, and 24/7 helpline support for all farming and business needs.
